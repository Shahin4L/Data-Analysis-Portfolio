# Data-Analysis-Portfolio
My Tableau Data Analysis Portfolio showcasing dashboards, charts, and stories from Superstore dataset.

Welcome to my Data Analysis Portfolio, where I showcase my journey in transforming raw data into actionable insights. This portfolio highlights not only my analytical skills but also my attitude, self-motivation, and ability to think critically, which I bring to every project I undertake.

**About This Portfolio**

This project is based on the Superstore dataset and demonstrates key insights into sales, profits, and customer behavior. Using Tableau, I have created a variety of visualizations and dashboards to communicate business intelligence clearly and effectively. As someone newly exploring Tableau and data analytics, this portfolio reflects my curiosity, rapid learning ability, and willingness to apply new skills to real-world data.

**What You Will Find in This Portfolio**
Charts and Visual Insights

**Overall Sales** – Understanding the sales trends over time.
**Sales by Quantity** – Analyzing product demand and volume.
**Best Performing Category** – Identifying which product categories drive revenue.
**Profit/Loss by State** – Visualizing financial performance geographically.
**Customer Segment by State** – Mapping customer demographics to business opportunities.
**Most Profiting Brands** – Recognizing high-performing products.
**Top 10 Sales** – Highlighting key revenue-generating products.

**Dashboards**

**Stats by Category** – Consolidated view of performance by category.
**Other Stats** – Supporting metrics and KPIs to understand operational performance.

Storytelling

**Data Story** – A narrative-driven visualization that explains the business insights, enabling decision-makers to understand patterns and trends easily.
